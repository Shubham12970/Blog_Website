Smart Solar Tracker with Power Monitoring

This project is a smart solar tracking system with integrated power monitoring, designed to optimize solar panel performance by ensuring continuous alignment with the sun. The smart tracker uses sensors to detect sunlight intensity and automatically adjusts the angle of the solar panel throughout the day, capturing the maximum possible sunlight. This real-time adjustment boosts energy generation efficiency by up to 30% compared to fixed solar panels.

The system includes a power monitoring feature that measures and displays energy production, usage, and efficiency data. Users can view real-time statistics on energy output, analyze performance trends over time, and detect any issues with energy generation. This allows for proactive maintenance and more effective energy management, as well as potential cost savings by optimizing output.

By merging sustainable energy production with smart technology, this project addresses the growing need for efficient renewable energy solutions. The combination of solar tracking and power monitoring provides a powerful, user-friendly approach to maximizing solar energy yield while enabling data-driven energy management for residential and commercial applications.
