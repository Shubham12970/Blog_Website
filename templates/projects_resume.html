Stock Price Prediction

The Stock Price Prediction project is designed specifically to predict the future stock prices of Tesla based on historical data and market trends. Using advanced machine learning techniques such as regression models, time series analysis, and deep learning algorithms, the system analyzes Tesla's historical stock data, trading volumes, and other financial indicators to forecast potential price movements.

By incorporating factors like economic conditions, company performance, industry trends, and even sentiment analysis of news related to Tesla, the model generates predictions for Tesla’s stock price in the short and long term. The machine learning model is trained on large datasets, continuously refined with real-time data, and evaluated for accuracy to provide reliable predictions.

This project serves as a valuable tool for Tesla investors, providing insights into future stock price fluctuations and helping them make more informed decisions. With its ability to focus specifically on Tesla, the system tailors its predictions to the unique characteristics of Tesla’s stock performance, offering more precise forecasts for those interested in trading or investing in the company.

Tech Stack:

The Stock Price Prediction project utilizes a range of technologies to efficiently analyze data, create predictions, and visualize results. Jupyter Notebook serves as the development environment, allowing for interactive coding, testing, and documentation all in one place. For data processing, Pandas is used to clean and structure the stock price data, making it easy to handle time-series information and perform necessary transformations. NumPy is employed for numerical operations, particularly with large datasets, allowing for high-performance calculations needed for machine learning.

To visualize trends and relationships in the data, Matplotlib and Seaborn are used. Matplotlib creates basic visualizations like line graphs, while Seaborn offers advanced statistical charts such as heatmaps and distribution plots, which help in exploring data patterns. Finally, ML Regression algorithms are utilized to predict Tesla’s stock price, analyzing historical data and identifying patterns to forecast future price movements with accuracy.


In this project, I first plots all different price charts and moving averages of the price on 7-day, 1-month, 3-months and 6-months basis. I also used four different algorithms to predict the price of the stock. These four algorithms are : Linear Regression, Random Forest Regressor, Gradient Boosting Regressor and Support Vector Machine(SVM).

I then, compared the different predictions and found that the Random Forest Regressor(RFR) Model prediction had the least difference between the actual and predicted price whereas Linear Regression Model had the most.

Microcontroller-Based Power Line Fault Detection

This project involves developing a microcontroller-based system for detecting faults in power lines, aiming to enhance reliability, safety, and efficiency within electrical distribution networks. The system leverages various sensors to continuously monitor electrical parameters, including voltage, current, and power factor, to identify and diagnose faults such as short circuits, open circuits, line-to-ground faults, and overloads. When a fault is detected, the microcontroller processes the incoming sensor data and swiftly isolates the problem area.

Once a fault is identified, the system triggers an alert, which can be communicated to maintenance personnel through a connected interface, such as a mobile notification, SMS, or web-based dashboard. This real-time alert system enables quicker response times, allowing operators to address issues proactively and prevent widespread power outages.

Moreover, the collected data can be stored and analyzed to identify trends in fault occurrences, which can help in preventative maintenance and infrastructure improvements. By automating fault detection and integrating it with communication systems, this project provides a robust and scalable solution for efficient power line management. The microcontroller-based design makes the system cost-effective and adaptable to both urban and rural power grids, contributing to smarter, more resilient electrical infrastructure.

Student Study Portal

The Student Study Portal is an all-in-one platform designed to support students’ learning and organization. Key features include:

  • Books: Access a digital library of textbooks, reference materials, and recommended readings for various subjects, available anytime and anywhere.
  • To-Do List: Stay organized with an integrated task manager that helps students track assignments, set reminders, and prioritize their workload.
  • Dictionary: A built-in dictionary for quick definitions, synonyms, and usage examples, making it easier to understand complex terms without leaving the platform.
  • Notes:A feature that allows students to take, organize, and store notes for each subject, enabling easy access for revision and study.
  • Homework Tracker:Keep track of homework assignments, deadlines, and progress, ensuring nothing is missed and deadlines are met.

Built with a user-friendly interface, the portal is accessible on multiple devices, ensuring that students can study anytime, anywhere. By providing a comprehensive suite of tools and resources, the Student Study Portal not only facilitates academic success but also fosters a collaborative learning community, encouraging students to excel and support each other in their educational journey.

By combining these features, the portal helps students stay organized, manage their studies efficiently, and enhance their learning experience.

Tech Stack:

The Student Study Portal is developed using Django, a robust Python web framework that powers the back-end of the system. Django handles critical features such as database management, user authentication, and overall application logic, making it ideal for building a secure and scalable platform. For the front-end, HTML is used to structure the content and define the layout, while CSS is employed to enhance the design, ensuring the portal is visually appealing and responsive

Smart Solar Tracker with Power Monitoring

This project is a smart solar tracking system with integrated power monitoring, designed to optimize solar panel performance by ensuring continuous alignment with the sun. The smart tracker uses sensors to detect sunlight intensity and automatically adjusts the angle of the solar panel throughout the day, capturing the maximum possible sunlight. This real-time adjustment boosts energy generation efficiency by up to 30% compared to fixed solar panels.

The system includes a power monitoring feature that measures and displays energy production, usage, and efficiency data. Users can view real-time statistics on energy output, analyze performance trends over time, and detect any issues with energy generation. This allows for proactive maintenance and more effective energy management, as well as potential cost savings by optimizing output.

By merging sustainable energy production with smart technology, this project addresses the growing need for efficient renewable energy solutions. The combination of solar tracking and power monitoring provides a powerful, user-friendly approach to maximizing solar energy yield while enabling data-driven energy management for residential and commercial applications.
